## 8. What This Means Clinically
- For the **lipid clinic:** TUDOR catches the cases DLCN drops — treated patients, cascade relatives, mixed dyslipidaemia
- For the **cardiologist:** TUDOR flags treated patients with T2DM where DLCN is below chance
- For **primary care:** no xanthoma examination required; deploys on routine lipid data
- **TUDOR does not replace genetic testing.** It refines pre-test probability for genetic testing.
